Once upon a time, in a dense, magical forest, lived a clever and nimble little cat named Mimi. She was about to move to a faraway snowy land. Meanwhile, in another part of the forest, there was a hardworking and loyal dog named Wang. He promised that within three years, no matter the difficulties, he would reunite with Mimi in the snowy land, just like Mimi's parents had done.

Mimi and Wang's relationship was always filled with laughter and playful games. They would often play-fight; Mimi would tap Wang's nose with her tiny paws, and Wang would gently hit Mimi's back with his tail. After each playful fight, Mimi would puff up and jump onto a high branch to avoid Wang, but she would secretly watch him from above. Wang, sitting below the tree, would look up at Mimi with his tail wagging excitedly.

To keep his promise, Wang started working very hard. He ran through the forest every day, helping other animals carry food, build nests, and clean up the forest. Whether it was the hot sun or muddy rain, Wang never gave up. He ran faster, carried more branches, and worked even when others rested. Finally, after many days and nights of hard work, Wang had gathered enough fruits and leaves to present as gifts for Mimi.

Mimi's parents had a deep friendship with Wang's parents, traveling between the forest and the snowy land. Mimi's mother had once traveled the entire forest with Wang's parents, pointing out the places they had visited and smiling at the memories. When Mimi's parents came from the snowy land, they brought beautiful ice crystals as gifts for Wang's parents, who carefully kept them and proudly showed them to other forest animals.

However, as the time to leave approached, Mimi became filled with doubt. She often sat on high branches, gazing toward the snowy land with her tail gently tapping the trunk, looking worried. During their last playful fight, Mimi suddenly stopped, stood in front of Wang with her ears drooping, and said softly, "Wang, can you really come to the snowy land within three years?" Her voice was filled with hesitation.

Wang stopped and looked at Mimi. Without saying much, he quietly walked to the side, picked up a branch, and sat under the moonlight, gently strumming their favorite forest melody. Mimi watched Wang's silhouette, her eyes shining with tears. Wang sang with a firm voice while strumming, "Mimi, no matter how hard it is, I will come to be by your side."

Mimi jumped down from the branch, walked over to Wang, and sat quietly beside him, gently nuzzling his cheek with her head. Wang stopped playing, extended his paw, and gently patted Mimi's back. They sat there together, gazing at the stars in the sky.

Three years passed quickly, and Wang kept his promise. In the snowy land, amidst the white snow, he and Mimi finally reunited. When Mimi saw Wang, she pounced on him, rubbing her cheek against his with all her might. Wang wagged his tail excitedly, circling around Mimi. They knew that no matter how much time and distance separated them, their love had never changed. Just like their parents, their story would become a beautiful tale, passed down forever.
